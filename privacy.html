Privacy


What data do you collect & how do you use the data?

When you invite Giveaway Bot to your server, we cache the guild object and channel objects. Additionally, we may also cache any user or member objects that Discord provides. Upon interaction with the bot, we may also cache your member and user objects if we do not already do so. We store this data solely for operation of the Giveaway service to prevent excessive API requests to the Discord API.


How can I contace you regarding concerns with the Giveaway service?

We are happy to resolve any data processing of security concerns you may have. The best way to get in contact with us is by joining our Discord support server and opening a Ticket in the #support-ticket.


How can I request my data or data removal?

Please join our Discord support server and open a Ticket in the #support channel. Unfortunately, we are unable to help you with these queries via external channels, as we would not be able to reliably verify your identity. We will handle your request within 30 days of it being logged.
